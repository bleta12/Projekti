Artists who made the world of art complete

Vincent van Gogh

Vincent took painting lessons in The Hague from a cousin by marriage, the celebrated artist Anton Mauve. Van Gogh felt his drawing technique was not yet good enough, so he also continued to practise fanatically. gave him his first commission: twelve drawings of city views in The Hague.

Leonardo da Vinci

While Leonardo da Vinci is best known as an artist, his work as a scientist and an inventor make him a true Renaissance man. He serves as a role model applying the scientific method to every aspect of life, including art and music.

Micheangelo

Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti Simoni, known mononymously as Michelangelo, was an Italian sculptor, painter, architect, and poet of the High Renaissance. Born in the Republic of Florence, his work was inspired by models from classical antiquity and had a lasting influence on Western art.

Pablo Picasso

The Spanish artist, Pablo Picasso, was a modern painter who through his unique artistic style inspired generations of artists to follow in his footsteps.
